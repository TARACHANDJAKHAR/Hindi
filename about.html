About Hindi Cell

The Hindi Cell at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat (SVNIT) is dedicated to the promotion and implementation of Hindi in official communication and cultural activities, as directed by the Government of India under the Official Language Act.

Our Mission

  • Promote the use of Hindi in administrative and academic work.
  • Encourage cultural activities in Hindi such as Kavi Sammelans, debates, and workshops.
  • Support staff and students in learning and using Hindi effectively.

Our Vision

To make Hindi an integral part of daily official and cultural life at SVNIT, in alignment with national language policies and cultural heritage.

Objectives

  • Organize Hindi workshops, competitions, and Hindi Pakhwada celebrations.
  • Publish official documents, circulars, and reports in Hindi.
  • Maintain resources for Hindi typing, translation, and literature.
